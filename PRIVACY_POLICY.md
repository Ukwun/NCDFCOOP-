# Privacy Policy - Coop Commerce

**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## 1. INTRODUCTION

Coop Commerce ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App" or "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing and using Coop Commerce, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:
- **Create an account:** Name, email address, phone number, password
- **Complete your profile:** Address, profile picture, payment information
- **Make purchases:** Order history, delivery address, payment method details
- **Interact with us:** Customer support inquiries, reviews, feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect:
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information:** Features accessed, pages viewed, time spent, clickstream data
- **Location Data:** With your permission, GPS location for delivery and mapping
- **Payment Information:** Transaction history (payment details are encrypted)
- **Analytics:** App crashes, performance metrics, user interactions

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

We may receive information about you from:
- **Payment Processors:** Flutterwave, Paystack (transaction confirmations)
- **Social Media:** If you sign in via Google, Facebook, or Apple
- **Delivery Partners:** Delivery status and tracking information
- **Business Partners:** Cooperative members, franchises, institutional buyers

### 2.4 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use:
- **Local Storage:** SharedPreferences for cart persistence and user preferences
- **Session Tokens:** JWT tokens for authentication
- **Analytics Tracking:** Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics
- **Push Notifications:** FCM tokens for message delivery

## 5. DATA SECURITY

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **HTTPS/TLS Encryption:** All data in transit is encrypted
- **AES-256 Encryption:** Sensitive data at rest is encrypted
- **Firebase Security:** Google's security infrastructure
- **Password Hashing:** Bcrypt or similar algorithms
- **Regular Security Updates:** Continuous monitoring and updates

### 5.2 Limitations
While we use reasonable efforts to protect information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 5.3 Your Responsibilities
- Keep your password confidential
- Do not share your login credentials
- Log out after using the App
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## 7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Coop Commerce is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we've collected data from a child, we will:
- Delete the information immediately
- Notify the child's guardian
- Take no further action regarding that account

For users 13-17, certain features may be restricted.

## 8. DATA RETENTION

We retain your information:
- **Account Data:** While your account is active + 30 days after deletion
- **Transaction Records:** 7 years (for legal/tax compliance)
- **Analytics:** 14 months (automatically purged by Firebase)
- **Communications:** Until no longer needed (12 months standard)
- **Crash Logs:** 30 days (Firebase Crashlytics)

You may request earlier deletion subject to legal obligations.
