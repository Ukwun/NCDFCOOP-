3-STEP INSTALLATION (5 minutes)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: ENABLE INSTALLATION FROM UNKNOWN SOURCES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Open Settings
2. Go to Security (or Privacy & Security)
3. Enable "Unknown Sources" or "Install Unknown Apps"
4. Tap OK/Agree

STEP 2: TRANSFER APK FILE
─────────────────────────
1. Copy app-release.apk to your phone's Download folder
   (via USB cable, email, or cloud storage)

STEP 3: INSTALL THE APP
───────────────────────
1. Open Downloads folder on your phone
2. Tap "app-release.apk"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Wait for completion
5. Tap "Open" (or find "CoopCommerce" in app drawer)

✅ DONE! Your app is ready to use.

INSTALLATION FAILED?
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

"Unknown Sources blocked"
→ Enable in Settings > Security > Unknown Sources

"App crashes"
→ Go to Settings > Apps > CoopCommerce > Clear Cache > Reinstall

"Can't find APK"
→ Check Downloads folder or ask to resend file

Still stuck? Contact support with your error message.
